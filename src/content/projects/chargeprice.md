EV charging in Switzerland is fragmented — different networks, different pricing models, different rates depending on your car. Before chargeprice.ch, comparing them meant guessing.

The tool lets you select your car and location, and returns a transparent price comparison across charging networks. No surprises at the station.

A useful tool for anyone driving electric in Switzerland.
